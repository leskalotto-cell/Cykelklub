About Northwest Cycling Club

Northwest Cycling Club (NVCK) is a community for everyone who loves cycling in and around the Northwest area of Copenhagen. We organize social rides, explore new routes, and welcome all levels – whether you’re a seasoned racer or just want to enjoy a relaxed ride with good company.

Our club is about fun, friendship, and discovering the best roads together. Feel free to join our Strava club, invite friends, and post when you’re heading out. See you on the road!
